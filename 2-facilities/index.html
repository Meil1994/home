Facilities

Kitchen


This kitchen will inspire you to cook.


It has modern equipment such as an oven, waffle maker, refrigerator, coffee maker, rice cooker, and induction stove.


It also has a lot of other utensils such as knives and cooking pans.


There is no perfect table setup without a complete set of plates and silverware. You are in luck! Our kitchen also has them.

Outdoor Pool


A perfect way to be refreshed on a hot day.


This kidney shaped outdoor pool has variety of things to offer.


It also comes with two sun loungers, swing chair, and a perfect view to complete the stay.


You can also request a dinner date setup to surprised your loved ones.

Outdoor Lounge and Dining Area


Nothing can beat a comfortable lounge and dining area.


It is a place to enjoy yourself with a companion.


We purposely removed the tv to encourage communication with your family and friends.


Instead, we add a lounge and dining area outside as a way for you to talk and reconnect with your loved ones.

Bathroom


Simple but stylistic bathroom.


A modern white design integrated with brass color.


It comes with a shower, toilet bowl with bidet, a mirror, face wash basin, towel rack, and most especially, free toiletries.


Not to mention, a very clean one.

Book Shelf


Tired of your problems? Feeling suffocated by everyone around?


Sit down and read books from this bookshelf.


It can be the most relaxing and magical thing you will experience.


As Stephen King said, "Books are a uniquely portable magic."

King Size Bed


The best possible way to end the day is a good night's sleep.


Meet our very own traditional king-size bed.


It has a clean bed sheet, a white blanket, 4 pillows, and couple of extras. It also has a bed extension for additional space.


Very comfortable, right?
